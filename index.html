About
BeeVerse is a play-to-earn game with RPG, PvP and city-building elements on Telegram! Our mission is to create an active community and contribute to the development of blockchain games. We are presenting an exciting world where tactical and strategic decisions play a key role, offering players a thrilling experience full of challenges and opportunities for earning real money rewards!

The primary goal of our project is to create an engaging game that provides strong motivation for any player’s growth
Benefits
You’ll be able to PvP with other players to earn our NCTR tokens, exchange in-game currencies for NFT and NCTR tokens, receive huge airdrops and become our OG player
Alpha
Boss Battles
In boss battles, players can earn real money rewards and get NCTR tokens! When a player defeats the boss for several consecutive days, they receive a streak, and his reward from the boss increases as well as chance of NCTR drop
Leaderboard
The leaderboard tracks the top players, and the best players in the alpha version will receive exclusive bonuses and drops in future! This creates a competitive atmosphere, motivating players to climb higher and higher in the ranks
Referral System
We have a robust referral system that rewards players for inviting friends. If a referral remains active and progresses in the game, the referrer receives a share of their earnings—namely, 5% of the balance of all their referrals
Earn Real Money
Become a part of our community! Defeat bosses, get NCTR drops, complete tasks, invite referrals and become the TOP player! Be among the first and shape the future of blockchain gaming

How to Make Money

Exclusive Token for Maximizing Profit
NCTR is a highly limited and sought-after token within our ecosystem, serving as the game’s sole premium currency. Players will use NCTR to purchase cases, sets, obtain unique gear and boosters, buy resources, and other useful in-game items. Some of the NCTR obtained by the project will be burned, continuously increasing the scarcity of this resource, meaning early token holders will benefit the most since the entire BeeVerse economy will be built on NCTR token
